Welcome to Greens Kitchen

Coffee and Friends the Perfect Blend

Who We Are

Based in North London, Greens Kitchen offers the perfect blend of casual dining in a relaxed environment. At Greens’ we would consider ourselves foodies (as in we love to eat) and we are passionate about sharing this with you! Our menu is freshly prepared daily, whether you’re popping in for a quick bite over our freshly brewed coffee or joining us for lunch it would be our pleasure to welcome you.
