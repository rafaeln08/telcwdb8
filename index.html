Who We Are

Based in Malagu Laguna, Cusina Mokka offers the perfect blend of casual dining in a relaxed environment. At Cusina we would consider ourselves foodies (as in we love to eat) and we are passionate about sharing this with you! Our menu is freshly prepared daily, whether you’re popping in for a quick bite over our freshly brewed coffee or joining us for lunch it would be our pleasure to welcome you.

They say you eat with your eyes!

At Green's Kitchen, we invite you to feast your eyes on our wonderful menu! From homemade cakes and bakes, tasty brunches to freshly prepared lunches and special dishes. We're bound to offer something that will catch your eye!



Home made

Our passion for food at Cusina Mokka is what makes us special, that’s why our menu is carefully prepared to include seasonal ingredients and dishes that are all made from scratch on-site. We believe in ‘you are what you eat’ and that food should be as vibrant as you are, we cater for a range of diets, preferences and allergies and are constantly evolving our offering to suit our customer needs. If you have any questions or feedback – we would love to hear from you.



Something New!

We know what our customers like, and how their tastes are changing, it won’t be long before we know what your order is before you’ve even told us! Sometimes you’ve got to shake things up and you’ll be tempted to try something new with our daily specials. The offering will always change based on what’s seasonal and fresh, bringing you the best ingredients we can get for a limited time!
